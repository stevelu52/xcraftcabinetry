Company Policy

Our policies for products, services, and customer commitments

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Introduction

Xcraft Kitchen & Bath is committed to transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction. These policies outline our guidelines for products, services, orders, and customer responsibilities.

Measurement and Design Responsibility

Customers are solely responsible for the accuracy of all measurements, sketches, drawings, and design specifications provided to Xcraft Kitchen & Bath for quotations or design purposes.

We do not accept liability for errors, omissions, or inaccuracies in customer-provided information. Customers must review and approve all final designs, layouts, and itemized product lists before placing orders.

Order Policies

Changes and Cancellations

Order changes or cancellations requested after processing are subject to a minimum 25% restocking fee. Certain vendors prohibit changes or cancellations once an order is placed; such orders are final and non-cancellable.

Returns

Most products are custom, assembled, or made-to-order and are not returnable, including:

  • Assembled cabinets, custom cabinetry, bathroom vanities, closets
  • Countertops (all materials)
  • Special-order or custom-color items

Exceptions: Stock fillers, panels, moldings in standard finishes and unassembled, unopened stock cabinets may be returned within 5 business days of delivery, subject to a 25% restocking fee. Returned items must be undamaged, in original packaging, with all accessories included.

Shipping and labor fees are non-refundable. Customers are responsible for return shipping to our warehouse.

Product-Specific Policies

Cabinetry Products

Wood is a natural product that expands and contracts with temperature and humidity. Small gaps (1mm–3mm) at joints or mitered corners are normal and not defects. Natural variations in wood color, grain, mineral streaks, or knots are inherent characteristics, not defects.

Engineered materials (MDF, particle board, veneers) may have minor surface or finish variations, which are not defects.

Closet Systems

Constructed with 3/4" M-2 grade furniture board and Thermally Fused Laminate (TFL) finish. Normal variations in color, texture, sheen, or edge banding are not defects. Custom closet systems are non-returnable under any circumstances.

Countertops

Natural Stone (Marble, Granite, Quartzite): Variations in color, veining, movement, fissures, pitting, and polish irregularities are normal. Thickness is nominal (±3mm), seams are required (±2mm) and not invisible. Customers must view and approve slabs before fabrication. Must be sealed regularly per maintenance guidelines.

Quartz: Man-made with natural quartz, resins, and pigments. Variations in tonality and pattern between lots are normal. Do not place hot items directly on quartz; always use trivets. Clean with non-abrasive cleaners, avoid harsh chemicals.

Stainless Steel Sinks

Wipe dry after use to prevent water spots. Use a protective bottom grid, clean with non-abrasive cleansers or baking soda paste. Avoid chloride-based cleaners (bleach, ammonia), steel wool, or abrasive sponges. For rust spots, use Bar Keepers Friend with a soft cloth along the grain direction.

Countertop Templating Fee

A non-refundable templating fee is required at scheduling. If you proceed with an order, the fee is credited toward your final balance. If you do not proceed, the fee covers travel, labor, and equipment costs.

Reschedule with 4+ business days' notice for no charge. Less than 4 days' notice results in forfeiture of the fee. Cabinets must be fully installed and leveled, farmhouse sinks installed and secured, all appliances/sinks on-site before templating.

Payment Terms

Full payment is required before production, shipment, or installation of custom orders. Unpaid balances accrue 5% monthly interest (18% annual) or the maximum legal rate. Customers are responsible for all attorney's fees and collection costs for unpaid balances. Returned checks incur a $50 fee.

Delivery and Storage

Ground-level delivery is included. Deliveries above ground floor cost $100 per flight of stairs. A responsible adult must be present to sign for delivery. Customers must assist with unloading.

Orders not delivered/picked up within 21 days incur $25/day storage fees. After 30 days, we may treat orders as abandoned, resell the goods, and refund the purchase price minus 25% restocking and applicable costs.

Inspect all items at delivery; note visible damage on the receipt. Hidden damage must be reported within 5 business days with photos. Installed or altered products are deemed accepted.

Warranty Information

All product warranties are provided solely by the manufacturer. Xcraft Kitchen & Bath makes no independent warranties, express or implied. Our liability is limited to the purchase price of the goods sold. We are not liable for labor costs, project delays, or consequential damages.

Installation Services

Installation may be performed by independent subcontractors who maintain general liability insurance. Xcraft verifies proof of insurance but is not responsible for subcontractor negligence beyond our own insurance coverage. Liability for installation work is limited to the cost of services rendered.
